Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

Raycon Global

Raycon Website

This website uses visual hierarchy very well. My favorite example is how they use bolder text in their product titles to draw the customer's attention to the primary purpose of each product. They put anything they deem most important in larger and/or bolder text than other elements.

Alignment

Youtube

Youtube website

This site uses alignment in many places. All the video thumbnails are aligned to a grid, and it gives the website a very organized appearance. The elements in the sidebar and header are all aligned well too.

White Space

IBM

IBM Website

I believe IBM's website makes good use of white space. All the text is padded well making it easy to read, and the amount of white space between elements helps establish which elements are closely related.
