Target Audience

Our target audience are citizens in the community who want to know what is happening in the community and business owners who want to get their business into the public eye.

Personas


Small Business Owner

A small business owner who wants to promote their business. They can use this site to market their business and consult with other business owners on practices that work to help them grow.


Family

A family in the city who enjoys participating in the community. They can use this site to discover what businesses in their area have to offer, and which ones might be more family-oriented.

Scenarios

  1. Are there any new restaurants in the city we haven't been to?
  2. Is there anyone in this city that already offers the same services I would be?
  3. What was the name of that little shop down the road?
  4. I need some advice for growing my business.
  5. I have to research businesses in my area for a school paper.
